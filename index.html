The Architectural Breakthrough

Our leadership isn't based on a single invention, but a trifecta of integrated breakthroughs on a single chip, creating an unbreakable competitive moat.

FMCW Architecture on a Chip

Description: The first to commercialize FMCW LiDAR on silicon photonics. Unlike ToF, we provide instantaneous velocity (4D data) for every point—a prerequisite for next-gen autonomy.

Business Impact: Delivers superior, interference-immune data straight from the hardware, solving critical safety failures like "blooming" that blind competitor sensors and eliminating the need for power-hungry computation to guess velocity.

On-Chip Beam Steering

Description: The holy grail of LiDAR. We steer a laser beam without any moving parts using proprietary photonic switches on the chip, perfected over six years of focused R&D.

Business Impact: Achieves radical simplicity and reliability. By eliminating mechanical components, we collapse a fragile, shoebox-sized system into a solid-state chip, slashing SWaP-C (Size, Weight, Power, Cost) and removing primary points of failure.

Unprecedented Photonic Integration

Description: Our chips contain 50x to 10,000x more components than typical photonic chips. We developed proprietary component libraries and CAD tools to design these massive, complete systems on a single die.

Business Impact: Unlocks unbeatable economics and a Moore's Law-like curve. Our fabless model enables massive scale with high yields and a clear path to >45% gross margins—an economic reality hardware assemblers cannot achieve.
